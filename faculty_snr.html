Dr. Sebastian N. Remello
Assistant Professor

nybinremllo@cusat.ac.in   senyre@gmail.com   Ph. no:8921952631
Research | Publications | Presentations and Talks | Teaching | Projects
CV | Group Webpage

Dr. received his Bachelor and Post Graduate degree in Chemistry from Sacred Heart College, under Mahatma Gandhi University in 2009 and 2011 respectively. He obtained his Ph.D in Applied Chemistry under the guidance of Prof. Harou Inoue, from Graduate School of Urban Environmental Science, Department of Applied Chemistry, Tokyo Metropolitan University, Tokyo. on “Artificial Photosynthesis Catalyzed by Silicon Porphyrins through two electron activation of water“
His teaching career began in 2017 as Assistant Professor in the Department of Applied Chemistry, CUSAT. His research interests include Development of porphyrin based Metal Organic Framework materials as catalyst for water splitting, Development of A3B porphyrins as potential ligands for Water Splitting Molecular Catalyst,

• Development of porphyrin based molecular catalyst for water splitting.
• Development of assymetrical porphyrins for enhanced electron injection in HECs(Hydrogen Evolving Catalysts)
• Development of optically transparent Semiconductor oxides for increased photon efficiency
