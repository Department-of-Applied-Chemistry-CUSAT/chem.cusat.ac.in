    Integrated Msc

  • Eligibilty Duration Nature Type intake
  • Candidates with a minimum of 75% marks at +2 level of education (Intermediate/CBSE/ICSE/HSC and all State Boards or Equivalent with science subjects (Biology, Chemistry, Mathematics, Physics) are eligible to apply. The programs are open to all students who have completed / expect to complete +2 stages with at least three of the four subjects (Biology, Chemistry, Mathematics and Physics) as their optional subjects with a minimum of 75% at +2 level. Preference in admission will be given to students possessing KVPY (Kishore Vaigyanik Protsahan Yojana) scholarship. They need not have to appear for CAT, but they have to apply for CAT through online and should pay the application fee. 5 years Non-professional Regular 60

    • Ph.D

  • Eligibilty Duration Nature Type intake
  • Candidates seeking registration for the Ph.D. programme shall hold a Masters degree in Chemistry from a recognized university with 50% marks in the qualifying examination provided that candidates belonging to SC/ST communities shall be eligible for concession up to 10% and provided further that candidates who have been teachers at least for the last five years in a college affiliated to any one of the universities in Kerala will be eligible for a concession up to 5% in the minimum eligible mark.
